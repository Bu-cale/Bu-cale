Core Projects Designed

As an architectural designer shaped by the coastal fabric of Mombasa and the fast economic pace of Nairobi, my design approach focuses on sustainable developments that cleanly promote spatial values, context, and structural reason.

I look to establish an intentional balance between rich vernacular design systems and clean modern technology. Using advanced 3D production frameworks, my goal is to explore spaces that thoughtfully prioritize local environmental context and individual well-being.
